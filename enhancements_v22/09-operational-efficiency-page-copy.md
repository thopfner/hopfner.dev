  - `Do you still use automation in this service?`
    - `Yes, where it helps the redesigned workflow hold in practice.`
  - `Is this only for larger teams?`
    - `No. It is highly relevant for SMEs and startup teams because workflow friction compounds faster when teams are lean.`

    - `That is normal. The first job is to identify the real constraints before deciding how to fix them.`
