# Future Section Recommendations

These are not required to publish the content pack now.

They are the next section types most likely to raise the ceiling of the site later.

## 1. Scheduler embed section

Why:
- the current booking path would convert better with a native scheduler or form embed section
- the `Book a Call` page should eventually feel like a complete conversion page, not just a content page with a CTA

Best use:
- `/book-a-call`

## 2. Results gallery section

Why:
- one `case_study_split` is strong, but an elite consultancy site benefits from a denser proof surface
- a results gallery could hold multiple anonymized or named wins with metrics, tags, and service-line labels

Best use:
- `/case-studies`
- home page proof teaser

## 3. Workflow diagram section

Why:
- elite sites in this category benefit from a visual before/after operating model
- the current section system explains well in text, but it cannot yet show a strong workflow transformation visual

Best use:
- home page
- workflow automation page
- process page

## 4. Engagement comparison section

Why:
- the current content can explain engagement models in prose, but a comparison section would make the offer ladder clearer
- ideal for contrasting review, implementation sprint, and ongoing optimization

Best use:
- `/services`

## 5. Credibility rail section

Why:
- once named proof is available, a stronger credibility section should combine logos, quotes, metrics, and service tags in one premium surface
- the current `social_proof_strip` and `proof_cluster` are useful, but a more integrated proof section would feel more elite

Best use:
- home page
- results page
