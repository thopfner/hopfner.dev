## Section 8

Section type:
- `faq_list`

Title:
- Workflow Automation FAQ

Content:
- Eyebrow: `FAQ`
- Items:
  - `Do we need perfect processes before starting?`
    - `No. The engagement is designed to help define the cleaner workflow while deciding what should be automated.`
  - `Do you only automate simple tasks?`
    - `No. The best work often involves cross-functional flows with multiple owners, systems, and exception paths.`
  - `How much team involvement is needed?`
    - `Usually one workflow owner plus access to the people who touch the process. The work moves fastest when ownership is clear.`
  - `Can AI be part of the workflow?`
    - `Yes, where it helps with summarization, classification, drafting, or contextual support. Standard automation still handles the repeatable structure.`
